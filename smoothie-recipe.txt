Ingredients:
 - 1 banana
 - 1 apple
 - 3 tablespoons milk
 - 2 teaspoons cinnamon
 - 1 teaspoon peanut butter
 - 5 walnuts
 - 1/2 package Greek yoghurt
 - 6 blueberries

Directions:
  1. Chop banana and apple into small pieces
  2. Add the fruit pieces to the blender along with the milk, yoghurt, peanut butter and the cinnamon.
  3. Blend until the mix is homogenous, then place into a bowl and add the hand-crushed walnuts and the blueberries
